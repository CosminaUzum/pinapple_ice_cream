About Pineapple ICE CREAM

Ingredients
heavy whipping cream
sweetened condensed milk
vanilla extract
canned crushed pineapple

HOW TO MAKE PINEAPPLE ICE CREAM
In a large bowl, add the whipping cream. With a hand or stand mixer, beat on medium speed until soft peaks form, about 3-5 minutes. Add the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla. Beat to combine fully. With a rubber spatula, stir in the crushed pineapple. Pour the ice cream mixture into a freezer-safe dish, cover, and freeze for 6 to 8 hours or until frozen solid.
